What is Discord for Mensans?

Discord for Mensans is the next step in the evolution of seamless global Mensa communication. Connect with Mensa members worldwide in the largest authorized online Mensa community.

Join Discord for Mensans Now

Imagine a place where Mensan conversations are no longer limited to a small group.

Imagine a place where Mensan conversations are no longer limited to a geographic region.

Imagine a place where Mensan conversations are no longer limited to one room on a specific platform that only exists to sell advertising.

Everything you do on other platforms can be done here–but bigger and better–by unifying the good things you like under one global roof in a house with 145,000 Mensans and built for you to easily converse on a topic with thousands then–with a single click or tap–engage a cone of silence and step into your own room to speak privately.

No more isolation by topic.

No more isolation by group.

No more isolation by platform or device.

No more defining your Mensan identity through a single lens.

Discord for Mensans is device agnostic, OS agnostic, platform agnostic and free.

Is this legitimate?

Established in 2022. This server is not a production of Mensa International, but it did receive authorization from Mensa International on 06 January 2023.

This server is entirely run by members of Mensa, but not necessarily employees. Any opinions expressed by server staff are entirely their own and in no capacity represent official stances of Mensa.

Site admin is Derek Crager, a US and International Mensa member.
